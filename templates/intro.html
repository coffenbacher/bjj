what is brazilian jiu-jitsu?

Brazilian jiu-jitsu, or BJJ, is a grappling-based martial art. It is famous for both its effectiveness in single combat and its complexity. The goal of BJJ is to force your opponent to submit ("tap out") with a joint lock or choke.

what is bjjweb?

BJJWeb is an attempt to index all of the open knowledge in BJJ, and surface it in an actionable manner for students of the sport. Actionable means everything from training logs to simple deep links in competition videos.

The knowledge graph of BJJWeb is an important innovation, since BJJ is an intensely cerebral sport. Every attack has a counter, every position has an escape, and every opening might actually be a trap. World chess champions have quit chess to focus on BJJ[1].

bjjweb flows

Most importantly, however, is the visual innovation BJJWeb calls "flows". These are visualizations of techniques and counters. In addition to providing a visual framework to build your game on, they let you easily visually identify holes in your game and areas to expand.
